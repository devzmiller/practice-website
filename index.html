The Unbeatable Squirrel Girl!

More Reasons Squirrel Girl is Awesome

Welcome to my website about the excellent Marvel superhero Squirrel Girl. She is a girl with the strength and speed of a squirrel. She's also a computer science student. She eats nuts and kicks butts.

You can read more about Squirrel Girl, AKA Doreen Green, on the Marvel Wiki. I think this website is a little bit overly busy; its three column layout packs too much content into too little space. But other than the superhero logo background it's what you expect a wiki to look like, so I wouldn't expect them to change things up much.

Reasons Squirrel Girl is Awesome

  • She has a big fluffy squirrel tail.
  • Squirrels can jump about four feet high. Which means Squirrel Girl can jump about 20 feet high.
  • She's a computer scientist and at one point defeats a villain by teaching him to count in binary on his fingers.
  • She likes puns.
  • She can talk to squirrels.
